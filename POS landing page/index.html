Core Features

🧾

Billing & Order Management

Fast POS billing (Dine-in, Takeaway, Delivery), table-wise order tracking, split bills, merged tables, GST-ready invoices, custom branding.

🍽

Menu Management

Add/edit items in seconds, categories, variants, add-ons, dynamic pricing, item availability, real-time sync across outlets.

🧑‍🍳

Kitchen Display System (KDS)

Live orders on kitchen screen, order status tracking, reduced paper, faster service turnaround.

📦

Inventory & Stock Management

Real-time stock tracking, low-stock alerts, ingredient-level usage, vendor & purchase tracking, waste control.

👥

Customer Management (CRM)

Auto-create customer database, order history, repeat insights, loyalty & discounts, personalized engagement.

💬

WhatsApp Automation

Auto bill messages, order confirmations, payment/invoice sharing, centralized WhatsApp, future: promotions automation.

📊

Analytics & Reports

Sales reports, best-sellers, peak hours, staff performance, profit insights, export to Excel/PDF.

🏪

Multi-Outlet & Admin Control

Manage multiple branches, central menu/pricing, outlet-wise performance, role-based access.

🔐

Security & Cloud Backup

Secure cloud hosting, daily backups, role-based permissions, data protection & reliability.

AI-Powered Insights (Coming Soon)
